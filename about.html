How Our Complaint System Works

Multiple Channels

Choose from various complaint registration methods:

  • Online portal (www.sindhpolice.gov.pk)
  • Mobile App (Sindh Police Complaints)
  • Helpline: 15 (Emergency), 1715 (General)
  • Police Station walk-in

Investigation Process

Transparent complaint handling procedure:

  • Immediate complaint acknowledgment
  • Assignment to investigating officer
  • Regular status updates
  • Final resolution report

User Protection

Your safety and privacy are our priority:

  • Confidential handling
  • Witness protection programs
  • Secure data encryption
  • Anonymous reporting options
